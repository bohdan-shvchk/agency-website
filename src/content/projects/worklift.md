WorkLift solves a real freelancer problem: Upwork's algorithm rewards proposal quality, but nobody teaches you how to write a good proposal. The platform analyses job posts, scores your proposal against what the client actually wants, and tracks your win rate over time.

The product was ready. The website wasn't.

With a Product Hunt launch 4 weeks out, the founders needed something that could do three jobs at once: explain a complex product to someone who'd never heard of it, capture email signups at scale, and start the conversion funnel toward a paid subscription.

The interactive demo was the key decision. Instead of screenshots and bullet points, we let visitors experience the product's core value in 30 seconds — before they'd even considered signing up. That single element drove over 1,200 of the 2,400 beta signups directly.
