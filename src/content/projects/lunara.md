Lunara makes pyjamas the way a good hotel makes a bed — with more care than you'd think necessary, and more effect than you'd expect.

The brand had strong word-of-mouth and a growing Instagram following. The website wasn't keeping up. Visitors would arrive from a beautifully shot Reel, land on a theme that looked like a template, and leave.

The core insight from the UX audit: trust was the bottleneck, not desire. People wanted the product. They just didn't trust the site enough to hand over their card details.

Every design decision in the rebuild was made through that lens. The size guide became a confidence builder. The checkout became frictionless. The post-purchase emails made customers feel like they'd joined something, not just bought something.
