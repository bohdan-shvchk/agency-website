Lunara Light is a health and beauty retailer selling across a broad range of categories — facial devices, massagers, depilators, maternity products, and more. The brand curates products from multiple suppliers under one storefront, positioned as a destination for at-home beauty and wellness.

The project started before any of that existed.

There was no store, no product catalogue, no supplier relationships, no payment setup. The brief was to build a business — not just a website. That meant starting with research: understanding the market, identifying what products were worth selling and at what margins, finding suppliers, and building the commercial infrastructure before touching a theme file.

The Shopify store launched first as a single-product experience — focused, fast, and built to convert. As the business grew and the catalogue expanded, we rebuilt the store architecture to support a proper multi-vendor, multi-category operation: new navigation, new collection logic, updated product templates, all while the store stayed live.

Payments, shipping across multiple fulfilment providers, and ongoing development support have remained in scope throughout. The engagement is still active.
