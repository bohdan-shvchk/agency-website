Healthcare websites carry more weight than most. A patient choosing a clinic is making a decision about their health — and the website is often the first impression of the care they'll receive.

VitalCare's old site communicated the wrong things: slow, visually outdated, impossible to use on a phone. For a clinic proud of its modern approach to medicine, the disconnect was costing them patients.

The rebuild wasn't just cosmetic. We fundamentally restructured how patients navigate to care. Instead of a list of departments, they now start with how they feel — and the site guides them to the right specialist. Instead of calling to book, they choose a time slot in 3 clicks.

The measurable results were significant. The less measurable one — patients arriving to their first appointment already trusting the clinic — matters just as much.
