Exonode solves a problem that costs e-commerce brands billions: returns that shouldn't have happened. Their ML model predicts return likelihood at checkout and surfaces interventions that reduce return rates without reducing conversions.

The product is genuinely impressive. The previous website wasn't.

A single landing page, no social proof, a contact form as the only CTA. Fine for getting early design partners — not fine for raising a seed round or winning enterprise procurement processes.

The interesting constraint: Exonode couldn't show their actual dashboard. Their data processing pipeline was the core IP, and screenshots would reveal too much to competitors. We solved this with custom Lottie animations that communicate the product's value without revealing how it works.

The investor deck page was a last-minute addition to the brief. It became the highest-converting page on the site.
