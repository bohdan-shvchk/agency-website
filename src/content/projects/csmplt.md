CSMPLT operates at the intersection of immigration law and tech career strategy. Their clients are senior engineers, researchers, and startup founders looking to relocate through talent visas. The stakes are high — and the site needed to reflect that.

The previous site was losing leads at every step. Visitors couldn't quickly understand which visa applied to them, the design undercut the premium price point, and the page speed was making Google ignore them entirely.

We approached the rebuild with one principle: clarity converts. Every section exists to answer a question the visitor has in that moment. No filler. No vague agency-speak about "unlocking your potential."

The result is a site that looks as premium as the service costs — and performs in both senses of the word.
