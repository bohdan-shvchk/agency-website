CARRER is a contemporary menswear brand built around curated seasonal collections and collaborative design. The store operates on Shopify and serves a customer base looking for considered, modern pieces.

The marketing team needed more control over how campaign content was presented on the storefront. Specifically, they needed sections that could accommodate both promotional banners and product cards within the same layout — and where every aspect of that layout could be adjusted: the number of columns, the width and height of individual blocks, the mix of content types.

The work involved building custom Shopify 2.0 sections with a Liquid block architecture designed around that flexibility. Each section exposes its grid and content settings through Shopify's native theme editor, so the team can configure layouts for any campaign brief without touching code.

The project is ongoing.
