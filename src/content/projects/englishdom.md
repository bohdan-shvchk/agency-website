Englishdom teaches English to over 500,000 students across Europe. When you're that size, a slow website isn't just a bad experience — it's a growth ceiling.

The challenge wasn't rebuilding a site. It was rebuilding it without disrupting the organic traffic that thousands of blog articles had accumulated over years on WordPress. One wrong redirect map and months of SEO work disappears.

We handled the migration with a custom redirect mapping sheet, validated against Ahrefs before we touched a single URL. Every article kept its slug, its canonical, its structured data. Then we improved all of it.

The course finder was the piece everyone was most excited about. Previously, users had to scroll through a long page of course cards. Now they filter by three criteria and see exactly what applies to them — with conversion rates to match.
